## What We Do
Jade Vault sources, transports, manufactures, and sells jade — controlling the entire value chain from mine to finished product. We have a 23 million kg supply agreement with the world's largest known jade deposit and are currently in active operations.

---

## The Market
- Chinese jade market: **£12.5+ billion annually**
- Jade revered in China for 6,000 years — status as a store-of-value asset
- Extreme value range: £4/kg (raw) to multi-million £/kg (high-grade)
- Large proportion of current supply is **illegally imported** — creating a legitimacy gap
- Severe impending **supply crunch** → inevitable price growth
